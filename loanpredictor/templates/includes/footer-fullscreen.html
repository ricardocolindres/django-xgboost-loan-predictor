© The following web app provides a machine learning model to categorize the risk that a small business has of defaulting on a loan secured by the United States Small Business Administration (SBA). This system was NOT commissioned by the SBA but is rather a personal project with a ML model trained on real data publicly made available by the SBA from all loans disbursed from 1984 to 2014.
