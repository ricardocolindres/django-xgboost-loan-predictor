Manage Risk

SBA Loan Predictor

This system is designed to aid credit agents in the process of analysis and assessing the risk associated with loans secured by the Small Business Association of the United States of America Government. The system employs a state-of-the-art machine learning model to classify new loan applications as low or high risk. Moreover, it provides the probability that the loan belongs to any of the mentioned categories. Finally, it also suggests the agent new optimized term (i.e., the period of time to repay the loan) and gross amount disbursed based on the ones contained in the submitted application to reduce the risk associated to defaulting on the given loan.

Manage Applications

SBA Loan Predictor allows credit agents to efficiently manage their time by automatically classifying new applications as low or high risk. Agents can then focus their valuable time assessing those loan applications that will yield the best results for all parties involved. Furthermore, the system tracks new applications and their outcomes and newly loaded loan data to retrain the model and keep predictions accurate throughout time.
